Advanced Tic-Tac-Toe — Rules

Objective

Win the big board (the 3×3 of 3×3 boards) by claiming three big boxes in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

Setup

  • The board contains 9 big boxes, each a 3×3 grid of small cells (buttons).

Turn rules

  • Players must play inside the currently highlighted big box.
  • If you play in the k-th small cell, you send your opponent to big box k.
  • If the destination big box is already won or full, the opponent may play in any open big box.

Winning a small (inner) board

  • Getting 3 in a row inside a big box wins that box.
  • The big box becomes closed and replaced by the winner’s symbol.

Winning the big board

  • Three won big boxes in a row → you win the entire game.
  • If all big boxes are taken but no row is formed → draw.
